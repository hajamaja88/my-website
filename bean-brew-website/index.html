Why visit Bean & Brew?

Fresh drinks daily

Enjoy espresso classics, seasonal specials, iced drinks, and loose-leaf teas made with care.

Comfortable space

Relax in a calm setting with soft lighting, free Wi-Fi, and plenty of seating for work or catch-ups.

Easy booking

Reserve a table online for study sessions, friendly meetings, or a quiet coffee break.

New this month: Pistachio latte, berry iced tea, and a vegan breakfast muffin.
